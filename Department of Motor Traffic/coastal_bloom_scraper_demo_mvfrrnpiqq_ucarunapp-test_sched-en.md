# How to Request Examination Certificates in Sri Lanka



## Overview



The Department of Examinations provides services for requesting official
    examination certificates. Eligible candidates can request certificates for
    examinations such as the **G.C.E. Ordinary Level Examination**
    and the **G.C.E. Advanced Level Examination**.



An examination certificate is an official document that confirms a
    candidate's examination results.



## Available Certificate Services



| Service | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Online Certificate Request | Allows eligible candidates to request an examination certificate online. |
| Certificate Status Check | Allows applicants to check the progress of a submitted certificate request. |
| Certificate Verification | Allows an online certificate to be checked using its certificate reference number. |
| Verification of Results | Confirms examination results but does not provide an official examination certificate. |



## Information Commonly Required



Applicants may be required to provide the following information:


- Full name of the certificate holder
- National Identity Card number
- Examination name
- Examination year
- Examination index number
- Contact number
- Email address
- Delivery address


## How to Request a Certificate Online


1. Open the official online examination certificate service.
2. Read the instructions displayed on the website.
3. Select the **Request Certificates** option.
4. Choose the relevant examination and examination year.
5. Enter the correct examination index number.
6. Enter the applicant's personal and contact information.
7. Review all entered information carefully.
8. Complete the required payment.
9. Submit the certificate request.
10. Keep the application reference number and payment confirmation.


## Checking the Certificate Status



Applicants can use the **Check Certificate Status** option to
    follow the progress of their certificate request.



The certificate request may display one of the following statuses:


- Application submitted
- Payment received
- Application being processed
- Certificate issued
- Certificate dispatched
- Additional information required


## Certificates for Local and Foreign Use



| Certificate Type | Purpose | Delivery |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Local Use | Used for education, employment, or other purposes within Sri Lanka. | Delivered according to the current local delivery procedure. |
| Foreign Use | Used for overseas education, employment, migration, or professional registration. | Delivered according to the selected foreign certificate service. |



## Examination Certificate and Verification of Results



| Examination Certificate | Verification of Results |
| --- | --- |
| An official certificate issued by the Department. | A document confirming that the examination results are correct. |
| Used as formal evidence of examination results. | Used mainly to verify reported results. |
| Requested through the certificate service. | Requested through the result-verification service. |



## Common Reasons for Delays


- Incorrect examination index number
- Incorrect examination year
- Differences in the applicant's name
- Invalid contact information
- Incorrect delivery address
- Incomplete payment
- Missing examination records
- Additional verification requirements


## Important Instructions


- Apply using the certificate holder's own personal and examination information.
- Check the examination year and index number before submitting the request.
- Provide a valid contact number and email address.
- Check the delivery address carefully.
- Keep the application and payment reference numbers securely.
- Do not request or use another person's examination certificate without permission.


> **Important:** Certificate fees, payment methods, delivery
>       procedures, and service conditions may change. Applicants should check
>       the latest official instructions before submitting a request.



## Required Documents Checklist



Before submitting the certificate request, applicants should prepare:


- National Identity Card or another accepted identification document
- Correct examination index number
- Examination year and examination type
- Valid mobile number and email address
- Complete delivery address
- Payment details
