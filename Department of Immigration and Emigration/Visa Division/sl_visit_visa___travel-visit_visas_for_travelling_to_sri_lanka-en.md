# Visit Visas for Travelling to Sri Lanka

# Overview

A visit visa allows an eligible foreign national to enter and remain in Sri Lanka temporarily for an approved purpose.

# Common Purposes of a Visit

- Tourism and sightseeing.
- Visiting family or friends.
- Short-term business activities.
- Medical treatment.
- Participating in meetings or events.
- Other approved short-term visits.
# Main Visit Visa Categories

# Before Applying

- Check whether a visa is required for your nationality.
- Select the correct visa category.
- Confirm passport validity.
- Check the permitted length of stay.
- Review current entry conditions.
- Check whether electronic travel authorization or other approval is required.
- Prepare supporting documents.
# Information Commonly Required

- Full name and nationality.
- Date of birth.
- Passport number and validity dates.
- Purpose of visit.
- Expected arrival and departure dates.
- Address and contact details in Sri Lanka.
- Travel and accommodation information.

# Tourist Visa Extension

Eligible tourist visa holders may request an extension using the current official process. The application should be submitted before the existing visa expires.

## General Extension Process

1. Apply before the current visa expires.
2. Use the official visa-extension service.
3. Complete all mandatory fields.
4. Upload the required documents.
5. Review the entered details.
6. Submit the application.
7. You must follow the instructions sent to you by the Department of Immigration and Emigration, which is the government office that manages visas.
# Important Conditions

- Remain in Sri Lanka only for the permitted period.
- Follow all conditions attached to the visa.
- Do not work when holding a visa that does not permit employment.
- Apply for an extension before the current visa expires.
- Use only official government websites and service channels.
- Keep copies of approvals and payment receipts.
- Leave Sri Lanka before the permitted period ends unless an extension is granted.

# Common Reasons for Delays

- Incomplete information.
- Incorrect passport details.
- Unclear document copies.
- Missing photographs.
- Incorrect visa category.
- Passport validity issues.
- Additional verification requirements.
- Submission through an unofficial website.
# Before Travelling

- Confirm that the visa or entry approval has been granted.
- Check that the passport details are correct.
- Confirm that the passport remains valid for the required period.
- Ensure that the approved visa category matches the intended purpose.
- Carry the necessary travel and accommodation documents.
