Landings Branch
The main function of this branch is to provide efficient and effective services to foreigners 
and Sri Lankans entering and leaving the country through approved ports of entry. In 
carrying out such duties, the branch ensures the national security of the country and 
contributes to economic development.
The Landings Branch of the Department of Immigration and Emigration's Head Office, 
located on the 6th floor in areas 'A' and 'B', conducts investigations as required and 
provides appropriate facilities and other support services.
