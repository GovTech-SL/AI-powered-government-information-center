# FAQs - Passport Application 

## Q1: Who can apply for a Sri Lankan passport?


A Sri Lankan citizen who meets the applicable requirements can submit a passport application.

## Q2: What documents are generally required for a passport application?

Applicants may be required to provide their birth certificate, National Identity Card, digital photograph acknowledgement and other supporting documents relevant to the application.

## Q3: Do I need to visit the Department personally?


Applicants may need to attend the Department or an approved regional office personally for identity verification and fingerprint collection.
